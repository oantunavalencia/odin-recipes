Chilaquiles

Description

This dish is a traditional Mexican recipe. The core part of the dish are the fried tortilla chips. These chips are then doused in either a red or green sauce. It is easy to throw add ons into the dish such as eggs, chicken, and steak.

Ingredients

  • 4 tortillas
  • 4 Roma tomatoes
  • 1 white onion
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 serrano pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of chicken boullion
  • 2 chile de arbol
  • 1 guajillo chile
  • Pinch of salt and pepper
  • 1/2 a lime

Steps

  1. Deseed and rehydrate chiles de arbol + guajillo chile in hot water. After 5 minutes, add to blender.
  2. Slice roma tomatoes, onion, and serrano peppers. Add to blender.
  3. Unpeel and crush garlic clove. Add to blender.
  4. Add dry ingredients (boullion, salt, and pepper) to blender.
  5. Squeeze lime into blender.
  6. Blend ingredients into a smooth sauce and transfer to a pan over low heat.
  7. Cut tortillas into small triangles.
  8. Fry tortillas in hot oil until crispy.
  9. Drain tortillas of excess oil on paper towels.
  10. Place tortillas into pan full of sauce.
  11. Add in any additional toppings and then serve. Enjoy!
