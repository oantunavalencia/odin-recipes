B.L.T (Bacon, Lettuce, Tomato)

Description

A simple yet popular sandwich. As the name suggests, it is made up of three primary ingredients. Bacon, lettuce, and tomato.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of sandwich bread
  • 1/2 tomato
  • 2 slices of lettuce
  • 4 strips of bacon
  • Pinch of salt and pepper
  • Sauce of choice

Steps

  1. Toast sandwich bread.
  2. Once toasted, smear sauce on both slices of sandwich bread.
  3. Slice tomatoes and place on bread.
  4. Season tomatoes with salt and pepper.
  5. Cook strips of bacon however you prefer. Once cooked, drain them of excess grease and then place on top of tomatoes.
  6. Place lettuce on top of bacon.
  7. Place the other sandiwch bread slice on top of the lettuce.
  8. Slice in half. Enjoy!
