Brown Sugar Maple Oatmeal

Description

A dish that consists of cooked oats in milk or water. It is sweetened with brown sugar and maple. A little cinnamon is added to contrast with the sweetness. Easy to make but filling!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of oats
  • 3/4 cup of milk
  • 2 teaspoons of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of maple syrup
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon

Steps

  1. Place dry oats in a bowl.
  2. Mix in milk and pinch of salt into bowl of oats.
  3. Place in microwave and heat for 2 minutes on high.
  4. Let cool and add maple syrup, brown sugar, and cinnamon.
  5. Enjoy!
